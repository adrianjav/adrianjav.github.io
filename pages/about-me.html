About me

Last Modified Aug 07, 2020

Hey there!

My name is Adrián and I am a PhD student working at the Probabilistic Learning group with Isabel Valera at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems in Tübingen, Germany. Previously, I obtained a master in Computer Science as well as a double bachelor in Computer Science and Mathematics at the University of Murcia, Spain.

My current research lays in the intersection of probabilistic learning, heterogeneous data, and non-convex optimization. Don't get fooled though, my interests are way wider and I am always happy to discuss interesting ideas!

Feel free to reach me out on Twitter or write me to adrian.javaloy@gmail.com
